Step 12-FinQuest Pro Ltd. Payment Assurance System Customer Guide

The FinQuest Pro Ltd. Payment Assurance Device

  1. Your Vehicle is equipped with a starter interrupt device and GPS tracking system (the “Device”). The Device is designed to give FinQuest Pro Ltd. the ability to disable the starter of the Vehicle and track its location.

  2. The Device uses wireless technology that allows Credit Horse Inc. to communicate with you & your Vehicle.

  3. The Device also contains a GPS tracking system that will allow FinQuest Pro Ltd. to know the exact location of your vehicle worldwide. The GPS feature may be activated should your account fall into default. It can also be used to locate your Vehicle should it be stolen. If you make your payments on or before your monthly due dates (as prescribed in your contract), the Device will not make its presence known.

However, should your payment be late, stopped, or returned to FinQuest Pro Ltd. marked NSF, FinQuest Pro Ltd.’s Payment Assurance System will instruct the device to:

  1. Either give a series of beeps (similar to your seat belt warning system) immediately upon the ignition being engaged or honk your horn in a series of honks. These warnings are the message to you that your loan is past due and you should immediately contact a FinQuest Pro Ltd.’s Customer Service Agent at 1-877-390-3864 to make payment arrangements to bring your account current.

  2. If you hear these past due warnings, immediately contact Credit Horse Inc. to make payment arrangements within 3 working days; the device is programmed to disable the Vehicle if payment is not made within such time.

  3. If, by the end of the third working day from the warnings being activated, you have not contacted FinQuest Pro Ltd. to make payment arrangements; your Vehicle will be disabled.

  4. The past due warnings will continue until such time as you have made your past due payment along with FinQuest Pro Ltd.’s late payment fee as outlined in your Finance Agreement.

  5. If your payment or payment arrangement has not been made by the end of the third day working day, FinQuest Pro Ltd. may disable the starter on your Vehicle, locate your Vehicle and begin repossession proceedings.

  6. At any point in this time period you may bring your account current, and FinQuest Pro Ltd. will once again enable the starter and give you use of your Vehicle.

  7. Should you default on your first payment with C FinQuest Pro Ltd.; the warning beeps will be activated three days prior to your payment date as a reminder to you to have funds in your bank account each month until your account is paid out.

Please note: At no time will your Vehicle be disabled when it is in motion. The Device will only disable the starter when the Vehicle is turned off.

Events of Default

Be aware that your account can be deemed to be in default for reasons other than past due payments, including but not limited to insufficient insurance coverage. Please refer to the Terms and Conditions contained in your FinQuest Pro Ltd. Finance Agreement.

Payments

Your regular monthly payment will be withdrawn from your bank account each month on the date described in your FinQuest Pro Ltd. Finance Agreement. If you are making a payment other than your regular monthly payment, you may do so by calling FinQuest Pro Ltd. at 1-877-390-3864. A FinQuest Pro Ltd. Customer Service Representative can assist you and give you instructions on how your payment can be made.
